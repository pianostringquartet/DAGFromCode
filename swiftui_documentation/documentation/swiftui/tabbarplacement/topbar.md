## Discussion

A tab bar can display as the top bar in [tab Bar Only](/documentation/swiftui/tabviewstyle/tabbaronly) on iPad and macOS, or [sidebar Adaptable](/documentation/swiftui/tabviewstyle/sidebaradaptable) on iPad.
